Kargin Haghordum (translated as “The Kargin Show”) is one of the most iconic Armenian comedy TV programs, first aired in 2003. Known for its sharp humor, clever sketches, and unforgettable characters, the show became a cultural phenomenon in Armenia and among the Armenian diaspora. It humorously reflected everyday life, social issues, and human relationships in post-Soviet Armenian society.
